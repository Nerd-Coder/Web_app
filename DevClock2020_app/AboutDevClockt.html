DevClock2020 is a very simple Timer specially whoever have addiction to starring at the computer screen for a very long time. it will help you out from starring at the computer screen for a long time.It follows the very popular "202020 rule" to make you alert about a continuous long starring at the screen. The app will make you alert after every 20 minutes and count a 20 seconds break for you.There are several musics for alarming. Anyway you can change. Care for you and for your precious eyes.
