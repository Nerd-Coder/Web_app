DevClock2020 Help:

After completing 20 minutes the alarm will start and won't stop until more 20 seconds pass. So when the alarm starts you should look something refreshing nearly 20 feet away from you until the alarm stops automatically.And then start playing again.

  • Click the "Start" button for start the timer
  • Click the "Stop" button for Pause the timer
  • Refresh the page for resetting the timer

For changing music check the "sounds" folder paste your favourite music and rename the sound file in the app's html file. It's copyright free So customize the the app whatever way you want and share :) .
